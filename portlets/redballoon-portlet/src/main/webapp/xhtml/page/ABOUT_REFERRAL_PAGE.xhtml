Help find the person who will write the proposal that wins the MIT Climate CoLab Grand Prize!

If you refer someone (directly or indirectly) who wins the $10,000 Climate CoLab Grand Prize, you’ll get a share of the $2,000 finders’ fee!

Here’s how it works: We’re giving $1000 to the person who invites the winner of the Grand Prize of $10,000, $500 to whoever invited the inviter, $250 to whoever invited them, and so on... It might play out like this: Alice enters her e-mail address below and we give her a unique invite link like http://CoLabRedBalloon.mit.edu/alice. Alice then e-mails her link to Bob, who also enters his e-mail address below so that we can give him a unique invite link like http://CoLabRedBalloon.mit.edu/bob, which he then posts to Facebook. Bob’s friend Carol sees the link, signs up to get her unique link, http://CoLabRedBalloon.mit.edu/carol, which she then posts to Twitter. Carol’s follower Dave then sees the link, signs up for the CoLab and creates (or helps to create) the best-ranked proposal, winning him the Grand Prize of $10,000. Once that happens we send Carol $1000 for inviting Dave, Bob gets $500 for inviting Carol, and Alice gets $250 for inviting Bob.
